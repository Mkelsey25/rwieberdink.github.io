About Me

Welcome! My name is Rebecca Wieberdink, and I am a Full Stack Web Developer and a certified science and math teacher. I earned a Bachelor's of Science in Education at Augusta University, and I graduate from Georgia Tech in October 2018 with a certification in web development. I am internationally certified in teaching English and grammar and have had the opportunity to teach speakers of other languages from 46 different countries. I am excited to combine my love of lexical analysis and algebraic thinking to build websites and applications that provide a riveting user interface (UI), effective functionality, and a meaningful purpose to individuals or a community.


Through my training in front-end technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Media Queries, JavaScript, jQuery, and Moment.js, I aim to provide optimal usability and responsive designs. I am sufficient in data analysis and dynamic web development through the use of server-side technologies, database software, and ORM's, including MongoDB and Mongoose, MySQL and Sequelize, Express.js, React.js, Handlebars.js, and Node.js.


Designing and developing websites and applications has proven to be challenging and motivating. I will continue to learn and code each day and provide useful and purposeful content to my clients.
